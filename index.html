"Information is only valuable once it's organized, accessible, and trusted."

Anthony Njau holds a BSc in Information Science and works at the intersection of data, systems, and structure — turning scattered information into something people can actually find, understand, and use.

His background combines a grounding in information organization and retrieval with a practical, hands-on approach to building and managing systems that hold up under real-world use. He's drawn to problems where clarity is the missing piece: messy data, disconnected processes, or tools that don't quite fit how people actually work.

This page is a living record of that work — updated as new projects come together.
